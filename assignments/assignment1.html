Sarah's Special Homemade Apple Cake

Recipe

What you'll need

  • 20 apples
  • 5 cups of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 8 cups of flour
  • 3 sticks of butter
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 800 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut all the apples into thin slices.
  3. Separate the egg whites and yolks.
  4. Mix the yolks and sugar with a whisk for 30 minutes to make a meringue.
  5. Melt the butter in a microwave by heating for 20 minutes.
  6. Mix the melted butter with the flour and transfer to a cake mold.
  7. Bake for one hour and make sure it's cooked.
  8. Take out the oven and remove a circle in it to fill with meringue.
  9. Fill it with the meringue and arrange the apple slices neatly.
  10. Put back in the oven for three hours for a nice burn and let cool off.
  11. Ready to serve!

Experience

Rating some ramen brands

I wanted to rate some ramen brands since there's no recipe here today. If you disagree, there's no comment button for you to tell me, not sorry!

Rank Brand Name Reasoning
2 Heel Ramyun The best brand of ramen noodles there is in terms of flavor. However, the only thing that takes it away from first place is the price. It's not bad but not too affordable at the price of $50 per package. Still, it's a popular brand for a reason.
3 Marusan Ramen The cheapest of the bunch but also a miss flavor wise. Though it brags several flavors, honestly, a bunch of them are pretty bad. I would not recommend buying these unless your wallet is in desperate need of help and you need a meal. It does make for good aesthetic pictures if you stack a bunch of the cups up though.
1 Bulwak Ramen Every flavor, they never miss. Although the flavor is a little below Heel's, this is the best ramen for your buck. Honestly might be a little better than Heel's with the flavors they've been producing recently, and a lot of people I know eat this after a long day's work.
