About

This site is for those who are looking for fresh new song recommendations! How it works is simple: all you have to do is complete and submit the form at the bottom of the page, and you'll receive an email with your personalized recommendations. However, if you're short on time, you can take a look from our Current Recommendations list, which has some songs you might like.

As of right now, most recommendations will be from the following broad three genres: 80s Japan City Pop, K-rnb, and K-pop. We're looking forward to expanding this list in the future.
