About Us

Custom Eats is a web application dedicated to providing those with dietary restrictions the ability to filter and accommodate their eating experiences. Here are the primary features we offer:

  • Customize your own filter by inputting ingredients and nutritional value ranges you want to filter out
  • Find restaurants and filter out menu items with your filter (if available for those restaurants)
  • Ability to place orders directly
  • Find out more information about food and dietary restrictions
